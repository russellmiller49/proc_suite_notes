PROCEDURE IN DETAIL

The patient was positioned in the supine position. The existing right-sided chest tube (inserted on 12/15/25) was identified and the dressing was inspected.

Using sterile technique, the chest tube was accessed. Intrapleural fibrinolysis was performed to address the multiloculated effusion.


Agent(s): tPA (10 mg) and DNase (5 mg).


Dose Number: 3.

The agents were successfully instilled through the chest tube. The tube was clamped/capped to allow for the prescribed dwell time according to protocol.

47-year-old male with complicated effusion underwent subsequent instillation of fibrinolytic agents (Dose #3).

The patient tolerated the procedure well.
