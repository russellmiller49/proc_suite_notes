PROCEDURE IN DETAIL

After the successful induction of anesthesia, a timeout was performed confirming the patient's name, procedure type, and procedure location. A CT Chest scan was placed on a separate planning station to generate a 3D rendering of the pathway to the target. The navigational plan was reviewed, verified, and loaded into the robotic bronchoscopy platform.

Successful therapeutic aspiration was performed to clean out the Right Mainstem, Bronchus Intermedius, and Left Mainstem from mucus. Ventilation Parameters: Mode: VCV; RR: 14; TV: 300; PEEP: 12; FiO2: 100%; Flow Rate: 10; Pmean: 15.

RUL Target (Anterior Segment RB3) Robotic navigation bronchoscopy was performed with the Ion platform using partial registration. The Ion robotic catheter was used to engage the Anterior Segment of the RUL (RB3). The target lesion is about 1 cm in diameter, and the catheter was advanced to 1.0 cm away from the planned target under navigational guidance.


rEBUS: Radial EBUS confirmed an eccentric nodule location. Features noted included continuous margin and absence of linear-discrete air bronchogram.


Imaging: Cone Beam CT (Cios Spin) was performed for evaluation of nodule location. 3D reconstructions were performed on an independent workstation and passed to the Ion platform. The robotic system was adjusted to the new targeted location based on this imaging.

Sampling:

TBNA: performed with 21G needle; 4 samples collected and sent for cytology.

TBBX: performed with alligator forceps; 1 sample collected and sent for pathology.

Cryobiopsy: performed with 1.1mm cryoprobe (6-second freeze); 6 samples collected and sent for pathology.

Brushing: performed with protected cytology brush; 1 sample collected and sent for microbiology.


BAL: Instilled 20 cc NS, return 10 cc; sent for microbiology.


Fiducial Marker: One 0.8mm x 3mm soft tissue gold marker was placed under fluoroscopy guidance.


ROSE: No evidence of malignant neoplasm.

RLL Target (Superior Segment RB6) Robotic navigation bronchoscopy was performed with the Ion platform using partial registration to engage the Superior Segment of the RLL (RB6). The target lesion is about 1 cm in diameter, and the catheter was advanced to 1.0 cm away from the planned target.


rEBUS: Radial EBUS confirmed a concentric nodule location with continuous margins.


Imaging: Cone Beam CT (Cios Spin) was performed with 3D reconstruction to evaluate and adjust nodule location .

Sampling:

TBNA: performed with 21G needle; 4 samples collected and sent for cytology.

Cryobiopsy: performed with 1.1mm cryoprobe (6-second freeze); 6 samples collected and sent for pathology.

Brushing: performed with protected cytology brush; 1 sample collected and sent for microbiology.


BAL: Instilled 40 cc NS, return 15 cc; sent for cell count, microbiology, and cytology.


Fiducial Marker: One 0.8mm x 3mm soft tissue gold marker was placed under fluoroscopy guidance.


ROSE: No evidence of malignant neoplasm.

LUL Target (Apical-Posterior Segment LB1/2) Robotic navigation bronchoscopy was performed with the Ion platform using partial registration to engage the Apical-Posterior Segment of the LUL (LB1/2). The target lesion is about 1 cm in diameter, and the catheter was advanced to 1.0 cm away from the planned target.


rEBUS: Radial EBUS confirmed an eccentric nodule location. Features noted included continuous margin and absence of linear-discrete air bronchogram.
